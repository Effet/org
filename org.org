#+TITLE: Org-mode

* Usage of Org-mode
** Shortcut keys
*** todo
#+begin_example
C-c C-t
#+end_example

*** open link
#+begin_example
C-c C-o
#+end_example

*** only view a little
#+begin_example
C-x n s
C-x n w
#+end_example

*** LaTex
Preview the LaTeX fragment at point, or all locally or globally.
#+begin_example
C-c C-x C-l
#+end_example
The images can be removed again with C-c C-c.
#+begin_example
C-c C-c
#+end_example
